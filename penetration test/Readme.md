# Argus Center Security Assessment

##  Overview
Conducted a comprehensive security assessment of the Argus Center to evaluate physical security, data protection controls, and operational risk. The assessment identified vulnerabilities, rated risks, and provided actionable recommendations to strengthen the overall security posture.

This project demonstrates practical experience in risk assessment, control evaluation, and security reporting applicable to banking and financial environments.

##  Objective
1.  Identify physical and data security vulnerabilities
2.  Assess effectiveness of existing security controls
3.  Rate risks based on likelihood and impact
4.  Provide prioritized mitigation strategies aligned to industry best practices

##  Scope & Methodology
The assessment covered:
- **Physical Security**: Access control, surveillance, visitor management
- **Data Security**: Data handling, storage, and protection practices
- **Risk Analysis**: Threat identification, vulnerability mapping, risk rating
- **Compliance Review**: Aligned findings to POPIA principles and ISO 27001 controls

**Methodology**: Risk assessment framework, control testing, stakeholder interviews, and gap analysis.

##  Key Findings Summary
| Area | Risk Rating | Finding | Recommendation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Access Control | Medium | Weak visitor verification process | Implement sign-in system and badge control |
| Data Protection | High | Sensitive documents not securely stored | Enforce encryption and access restrictions |
| Surveillance | Low | CCTV blind spots in entry areas | Reposition cameras and add monitoring |

*Full details, evidence, and remediation plan are in the attached report.*

##  Recommendations
Aligned to POPIA, ISO 27001, and banking security standards:
1.  **Strengthen Access Controls**: Role-based access and visitor logging
2.  **Improve Data Governance**: Encrypt sensitive data and restrict access on a need-to-know basis
3.  **Enhance Monitoring**: Regular audits and security awareness training
4.  **Incident Response**: Document procedures for reporting and responding to security events

##  Banking Relevance
Security assessments are core to banking risk and cybersecurity teams. 
This project demonstrates ability to identify control gaps, assess risk to customer data and assets, and recommend mitigations that support regulatory compliance and reduce financial/operational exposure.
